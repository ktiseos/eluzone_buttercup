**Gymnosperms** are a [[011/02/Taxonomic Clades\|clade]] of [[Seed Plants\|seed plants]]. Gymnosperms produce [[Seeds\|seeds]] for [[Sexual Reproduction\|sexual reproduction]]; however, compared to their [[011/02/Sister Groups\|sister]] clade, [[011/02/Angiosperms\|angiosperms]], gymnospermsâ€™ seeds are not enclosed in a protective structure, such as a [[011/02/Fruit\|fruit]].

Gymnosperms include conifer plants such as redwoods, sequoias, and pine trees. All gymnosperms are [[011/02/Woody Plants\|woody plants]]; there are no herbaceous gymnosperms.
