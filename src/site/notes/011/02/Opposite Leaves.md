The [[011/02/Leaves\|leaves]] of a [[011/02/Vascular Plants\|vascular plant]] are **oppositely** attached if specifically two leaves are attached at each [[011/02/Plant Nodes\|node]] on the [[011/02/Stems\|stem]].
