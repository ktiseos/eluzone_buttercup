The [[011/0209/Leaves\|leaves]] of a [[011/0209/Vascular Plants\|vascular plant]] are **oppositely** attached if specifically two leaves are attached at each [[011/0209/Plant Nodes\|node]] on the [[011/0209/Stems\|stem]].
